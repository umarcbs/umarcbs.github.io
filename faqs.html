Contact Us
Can I order in bulk?

Yes you can, provided your order has not been shipped. Mail us at alhudadesigns@gmail.com to change your order information. Please note that after your order is shipped you cannot change any details on your order.

Can I change my order information?

Mail us at alhudadesigns@gmail.com to change your order information. Please note that after your order is shipped you cannot change any details on your order.

Can I change the delivery address?

Call or Mail us to change your order information. Please note that after your order is shipped you cannot change any details on your order.

Can I customize the product?

Yes you can and your order will be placed as per your choice. Please mention preferable colors, sizes, finish and other details.

Can I get a discount Coupon?

If you have received any discount Coupon on your email, you may use the same. Apart from that, there are special discount offers available for bulk orders.

Can I place an Order over the Phone?

Yes you can. We only receive orders on phone.

What is the ordering time?

You can call us for orders only between 09:00 am to 06:00 pm.
