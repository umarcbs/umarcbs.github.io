What does MAPs stand for?

MAPs stands for Medicinal & Aromatic Plants.

What is this database about?

This database is all about the Medicinal & Aromatic Plants of Jammu & Kashmir. It contains details about the plants of J&K like name of plant, family, etc.

What attributes are used in this database?

The attributes/features that we used in this database are:

  • Botanical Name Author
  • Vernicular Name
  • Family
  • Habit
  • Part(s) used
  • Status
  • District, etc.
    • Click here to check full details about the attributes of the database.

    How many families are included in this database?

    A total of 102 families are included in this database.

    A total of 833 species are included in this database.
